ASSOCIATE CREATIVE DIRECTOR

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

Creativity

  • Build client’s business objectives into unique brand experience.
  • Manage creativity of the projects from concept until execution.
  • Lead brainstorming sessions and creation of strategic concepts for advertising campaign.
  • Lead creative team to create exceptional visual style & tone for advertising campaign.
  • Actively work with internal teams to build creative proposal and participate in new business pitches
  • Present creative proposal to clients in a compelling and clear manner. 

Production:

  • Take creativity lead role in pre-production/production/post-production of the photo/video shooting.
  • Be able to discuss with Director/DOP to make best results.

Team management:

  • Develop leadership skills through mentoring and training
  • Qualify, prioritize and delegate workloads for creative team
  • Mentor team of creative members - manage their performance and career development
  • Create and improve the working process for creative team.

Business

  • Partner with the Account and cross-functional team leaders to manage senior level clients
  • Develop up new business.
  • Work with Client Service department to balance the financial benefits among client expectation, production feasibility and budget, make sure the advertising campaign execution is always on budget.
  • Report to Creative Director

 

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Be a Problem Solver with positive “I CAN DO” attitude. April believes experience can be learnt but negative “I CAN NOT DO” attitude will not lead us all far.
  • At least 5+ years of experience in advertising.
  • Ability to command different visual styles/tones using latest Adobe creative software.
  • Be stable under pressure and have strong sense of urgency.
  • Be highly responsible, collaborative and self-motivated
  • Must be effective at multi-tasking, time management.
  • Passion for new technologies and emerging trends
  • Good English communication skills.

 

BENEFITS:

  • A motivated & open working environment.
  • Salary: attractive & negotiable based on skills and experience.
  • 24/7 health insurance.
  • To be trained necessary skills.
  • Live and work in April+ culture.

 

Interested candidates, please send your package of application including CV, Portfolio & any information if you would like us to see to hr@april.com.vn

Please note that only shortlisted candidates will be informed for the next round.
